Our Mission: Offering You a Better Furniture Shopping Experience

When you need quality Ashley furniture and other fine home furnishings, save a lot and enjoy a better experience every time at Best Buy Furniture & Mattress. Owner Eden Lulu and his seasoned staff offer you the perfect balance of extensive product knowledge and a no-pressure sales floor. You'll appreciate the difference every time you shop for pieces and sets at our Pennsauken, NJ and Philadelphia, PA furniture stores. We set a high standard for ourselves because we realize you're more than just one sale. We hope to provide abundantly for all your home furniture and mattress needs, even to the next generation of your family. A tall order for a furniture shop? Not for Eden and his crew. Our home furniture stores strive to exceed your expectations for pieces and collections both stylish and eminently affordable, as well as state-of-the-art, lab-proven mattress solutions. That's why local hardworking households have made Best Buy Furniture & Mattress their watchword for quality and savings, when you can expect friendly service. So when you want to upgrade to new pieces, sets, and mattresses for far less than what the other places charge, be sure to bookmark this furniture store site to get the latest info, then stop by any of our stores, where our “people first” staff await to assist.

One of our furniture store's goals is always to find unique, well-crafted items from around the globe and to bring those to you for the best possible price. At Best Buy Furniture & Mattress, the only limit is your imagination, never your budget!
